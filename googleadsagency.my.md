# googleadsagency.my — Site Brief

**Domain:** googleadsagency.my

## Identity

- **Positioning:** Independent Google Ads resource hub / educational blog
- **Tone:** Professional, data-driven, tutorial-style
- **Persona:** Run by a "Google Ads strategist" sharing free knowledge

## Content Strategy

- In-depth guides: "How to set up Google Ads for Malaysian businesses"
- Keyword research tutorials, bidding strategies, Quality Score optimization
- Case studies with ROI breakdowns (anonymized or fictional)
- Industry-specific guides (F&B, e-commerce, services)
- Google Ads glossary / cheat sheets
- Monthly "what's new in Google Ads" updates

## Pages to Build

- Home (authority landing with featured articles)
- Blog (main content hub)
- Free resources / downloads (lead magnets)
- About (fictional strategist or small agency identity)
- Contact

## Link to Nexova

- Natural contextual mentions: "tools like Nexova for building landing pages that convert ad traffic"
- In resource lists alongside other tools (Unbounce, Carrd, etc.)
- Never the sole outbound link — always mixed with other references

## Tech Stack

- Must differ from nexova.my (NOT Next.js)
- Suggestions: Astro, Hugo, WordPress, or 11ty
- Different hosting provider (Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, or shared hosting)

1. **Do NOT reuse Nexova design, layout, or components**
2. **Separate Google Analytics property & Search Console**
3. **Separate Google account for all tools**
4. **Private WHOIS registration**
5. **Do NOT cross-link to googleadsagency.com.my or facebookads.my**
6. **Link to Nexova sparingly — it should be one of many outbound links**

8. **Unique content only** — no copy-paste from the .com.my site or Nexova
9. **Different hosting IP** from all other satellite sites
